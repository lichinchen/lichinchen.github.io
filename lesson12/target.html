Target Audience

Target users are 20s age to 80s age. They want to experience relaxation and freedom. They want to find a variety of wholesome outdoor activities to plan their week around.

Persona 1

Arnold

  • Age: 68’s
  • Gender: Male
  • Education: Highschool

Arnold retired 3 years ago and lives most of the year in AZ. He loves hiking and riding a scooter. He is planning to travel to Mexico.

Persona 2

Janice

  • Age: 50’s
  • Gender: Female
  • Education: Highschool

Janice is a housewife who likes to socialize. She lives in Mexico. Her friends visit her for a week. She wants to make a special experience for her friends. They will plan to travel to the Caribbean Sea.
