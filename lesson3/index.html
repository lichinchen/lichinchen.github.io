Purpose

This site is Gardeners what want to get the latest up to date weather information for gardening. We have five goals for starting this site:

  1. Know how much to water: Watering the correct amount not only benefits your vegetables but also help you save on your water bill
  2. Know when to plant: knowing the weather condition will help you determine the best time to put seeds in the ground for proper germination.
  3. Avoid frost damage: The last spring frost and the first autumn frost are critical for frost-sensitive plants.
  4. Prevent wind damage: Up to date wind monitoring can let you know if you need to protect your plants.
  5. Plan gardening activities: Accurate forecasts can help you manage your gardening activities from seeding to harvesting.
